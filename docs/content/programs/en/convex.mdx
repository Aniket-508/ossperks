## Perks

- **Free Convex Pro**: Free Convex Pro plan for one team with built-in monthly Pro resource limits.
- **Convex Swag**: Stickers, shirts, and gear to rep your stack.
- **Community Support**: Direct access to the Convex team and developer community when you need help.

## Eligibility

- Must be an actively maintained and fully open-source project.
- Must be built with Convex or planning to integrate Convex.
- Must support the developer ecosystem through tools, libraries, or resources.

## How to apply

1. Apply through the Convex OSS program page.
2. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis with responses within 60 days.
